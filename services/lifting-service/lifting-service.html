How it works

Where do we go?

At Galilee Freight, we are proud to offer comprehensive moving services for customers in Auckland, Hamilton, Whangarei, Napier, and Hastings. No matter where you are located in these cities, we can help you seamlessly relocate to any destination within New Zealand.
With our team of experienced professionals and reliable moving equipment, we are committed to making your move stress-free and efficient. Contact us today to learn more and schedule your move with Galilee Freight.

The process:

  • Contact Galielle Freight to schedule a consultation and receive a quote for your move.
  • Pack and organize your belongings in preparation for the move.
  • Galielle Freight will provide packing materials and assistance if requested.
  • The team will arrive on the designated moving day to load your belongings onto the truck.
  • The truck will transport your belongings to your new city.
  • Upon arrival, the team will unload the truck and assist with placing your belongings in your new home.
  • Galielle Freight will provide any additional services requested, such as unpacking and organizing.
  • The team will complete a final walk-through to ensure everything has been completed to your satisfaction.

Affordable solutions:

We believe that moving should be accessible and affordable for everyone. That's why we offer a range of moving solutions to fit every budget and need. From full-service moves to a la carte options, we have something for everyone. Contact us today to learn more and receive a customized quote for your move with Galilee Freight.
