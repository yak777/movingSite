FAQs

How do I get a quote for my move?

You can get a quote for your move by contacting us directly or by filling out an online quote form on our website. Be sure to provide as much information as possible, including the size of your home, the distance of the move, and any special requests or considerations you may have.

What is included in the cost of a move?

The cost of a move typically includes the cost of labor, packing materials, and transportation of your belongings. It may also include additional services such as packing and unpacking, loading and unloading, and storage. Be sure to ask about any additional fees that may apply, such as for heavy or fragile items, or for extra insurance coverage.

Can I pack my own belongings?

We offer a full-service option where we will pack and move all of your belongings for you. However, this is entirely optional and you can pack your own items, either with our packing materials or with your own. Be sure to discuss this with us to see what we can do for you.

What happens if something gets damaged during the move?

We can offer insurance coverage for damages that may occur during the move. Be sure to ask about the specifics of the coverage and what steps you need to take to file a claim in the event of any damages. It is also a good idea to take detailed inventory of your belongings and document their condition before the move to make it easier to identify any damages that may occur.
